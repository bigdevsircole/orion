Frequently Asked Questions

Is Orion Manager safe to use?

Yes, absolutely. We use bank-level encryption, never store your credentials, and are fully GDPR compliant. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

Can I recover deleted activity after deletion?

No. Deletion through Orion Manager is permanent. Once deleted, Google activity cannot be recovered. This is by design for maximum privacy.

How long does the deletion process take?

Most deletions complete within minutes, depending on the amount of data. Large deletion jobs may take up to 30 minutes.

Do you store my Google password?

Never. Orion Manager uses OAuth authentication, meaning Google handles your credentials securely. We never have access to your password.

What happens if the deletion fails?

Our support team will assist you immediately. All plans include email support, with Enterprise receiving 24/7 priority assistance.

Can I delete data from multiple accounts?

Yes! Professional and Enterprise plans support multi-account management. Basic plan covers one account at a time.
